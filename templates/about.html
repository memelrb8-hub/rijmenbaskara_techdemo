Established in 2020

RIJMEN & BASKARA creates Bespoke Models for Historical Preservation and Visual Display

Originating from Jakarta, Indonesia, Rijmen & Baskara focuses on creating meaningful and artistic models of subjects at scale in order to preserve its importance in a tangible form.

Rijmen & Baskara offers bespoke models with a unique emphasis in artistic liberty while still maintaining visual realism in order to honor the original vision and intent of the creators of the subject.
